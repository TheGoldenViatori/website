Chapter Three - Composure and Fortitude

As I walk to school I worry about other people like always. No one can be calm anymore. Whenever anyone feels upset or angry they take it out on others especially me. Whenever I tell them to stop treating me how they are, they make excuses. I once read in a book about psychology, any apology where someone says “I’m sorry but-” Isn’t sincere. “I’m sorry but I feel upset.” “I’m sorry but I’m really stressed” “I’m sorry but I can’t deal with people.” That’s all I hear after someone acts horribly and somehow their excuses justify it, somehow, it’s okay and if I question that I'm the inconsiderate person in this situation.

I’m always upset, I’m always stressed, I’m never in the mood to deal with people but I’m always nice to everyone. Maybe it’s time I withdraw from everyone, for good. I’m too empty to talk to the friends I actually care about, I’m just a burden for them, It’s selfish to stay around them.

At lunch I go to the emptiest part of the school and sit under a tree. I’m the only one here, just how I want it. I grab my book out and start reading, I have nothing else to do. After school I go to my spot in the park and I read my book. I still haven’t said a single word to anyone today. Before I realise it, It’s getting dark.

I get back home and fall onto my bed. I opt to skip dinner and sleep. I feel so tired yet, I can’t sleep. So many thoughts fly in and out of my head. I have all these people I can reach out to but I’m still by myself.

My phone buzzes and I freak out. I always had my phone on silent and only got notifications from one person, my old boyfriend. I haven't talked to him in such a long time. My brain thinks of every possible reason he could be messaging me as I reach for my phone.

"Hey Illuna, You seem really alone, you know I'm here if you need to talk, right?"

I look at my screen with shock. I thought he hated me. He has every reason to after all. I message him back:

“Don’t worry about me, I am fine (=.”

I throw my phone onto my desk, it slides off the otherside, falling to the ground. I then fall into my bed staring up at the ceiling. I at least know that I hate him.

I Never wrote past this, doubt I ever will
